    • Ultimate TicTacToe

    This game was made in my studio Adamax. Each small grid (3x3) is called a small board, while the larger one (9x9) is referred to as the large board. The first moves belong to the crosses. They can play in any of the cells. With their move, they "send" the circles to one of the small boards. For example, if crosses make a move in the bottom right corner of a small field, circles must make a move on the bottom right board, in turn, "sending" crosses to one of the fields.
